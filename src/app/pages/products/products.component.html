Saral Stone is one of the leading stone products manufacturers and suppliers that deals into natural stone, granites, sandstones and marbles, quartzite stone and artificial marbles. Life is blown into stone pieces at Saral Stone Laboratory for it has finest designers from France, Italy and Dubai. Advanced technology is used to reveal the latent beauty of stone along with performing additional research on lighting technology in the field of stone design.

The extended research of Saral Stone holds a paramount importance and has resulted in an unprecedented offering of modular architectural solutions that are ready for use and created in the most durable natural material used by people with highly environment-friendly processing techniques.
